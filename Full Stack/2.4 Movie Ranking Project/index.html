My Top Three Movies

Teenage Mutant Ninga Turtles II: The Secret of the Ooze

This is my absolute favorte movie ever made. Is it an excellent piece of cinematic history that carries you through an elaborate and quite unique story that will leave the viewer totally engaged? No. Is it still AWESOME? YES!

Ex Machina

I believe this is a must watch for anyone pursuing a career in machine learning or artificial intelligence. It is gripping, great visually, and just an overall good time.

Vampire in Brooklyn

This is a guilty pleasure of mine. It combines one of my favorite sci-fi creatures and the culture of my people. It isn't a great movie, and the writting could use a lot of work, but it is enjoyable nonetheless. Eddie Murphy and Angela Bassett's acting carry this movie higher than it should be listed in my rankings.
